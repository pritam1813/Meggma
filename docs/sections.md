# Section Guide

Meggma includes over 30 customizable sections. Here is a guide to the key sections.

## Hero Sections
- **Hero**: Standard full-width hero banner with text overlay.
- **Hero Two**: Split layout hero with image and text.
- **Background Video**: Full-width video background with text.

## Product Sections
- **Product**: main product page section.
- **Product Card**: Reusable product display card.
- **Collection**: Grid view of products in a collection.

## Marketing Sections
- **Image Banner**: Large banner image with call-to-action.
- **Marquee Section**: Scrolling text for announcements.
- **Testimonials**: Display customer reviews.
- **Blog Posts**: Featured articles from your blog.

## Utility Sections
- **Contact Form**: Standard contact form.
- **Support Form**: Specialized support request form (New!).
- **FAQ**: Accordion-style frequently asked questions.

## Customizing Sections
Each section has unique settings. Click on a section in the Theme Editor to:
- Change colors and fonts.
- Toggle visibility of elements.
- Adjust spacing and layout.
